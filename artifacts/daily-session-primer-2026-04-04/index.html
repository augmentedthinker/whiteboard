In that sense, this morning may be less about choosing a forever-path than choosing the next live chapter with enough seriousness that the weekend does not dissolve into elegant adjacency work.

Interpretive read: the real bottleneck is no longer imagination. It is whether the system can pick one or two lanes and let them place demands back on life.
