Storyboard — 4 Act Arc

I
The Ashen Bell

At age twelve, Kaelith rang a temple bell that answered with dragonfire. The village survived; his family did not.

II
Exile and Study

He wandered ruin to ruin, learning to control raw power through glyphs, breath discipline, and old draconic chants.

III
The Pact Refused

A crimson wyrm offered dominion in exchange for obedience. Kaelith refused, choosing purpose over power.

IV
Quest of Emberglass

Now he seeks the Emberglass Crown, an artifact said to bind flame to will—before warlords awaken it first.

Session Hook

The party arrives at Black Lantern Keep as skyfire storms begin. Kaelith senses the same resonance from the Ashen Bell—meaning the Emberglass Crown is already near.
