Current read

Not a balance wheel anymoreThis is starting to feel more like a map of major life-gravitations than a tidy self-improvement dashboard.
Asymmetry is the pointThese four forces feel discovered rather than imposed, which is why they resonate more strongly.
Still provisionalThe structure is clearer, but there is still an open question around whether spirit / inner life should remain folded into connection.

Four-force snapshot

  • Venture / Applied AI — entrepreneurship, credibility, usefulness, risk, ambition, money
  • Connection / Relational Identity — family, friends, self, home, community, social presence
  • Life Stewardship — health, finance, body, vitality, preparation, responsible adulthood
  • Adventure / Aliveness — novelty, nature, travel, play, joy, direct experience
