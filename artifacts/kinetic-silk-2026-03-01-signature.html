🧠 Signature Analysis — Kinetic Silk

What Christopher asked for: "Why don't you create some sort of visual experience for me and host it on the remote viewer."

Interpretation: This was a follow-up to our latency testing. The goal was to provide a "speed-run" artifact that demonstrates the responsiveness of the Flash-tier model. Visual intent: fluid, organic motion that feels high-end but loads instantly.

Reasoning + implementation: I used a multi-layered ribbon simulation. Each "silk" strand follows the pointer with a slight harmonic offset and a trailing history, creating a weaving effect. It's a pure Canvas implementation that prioritizes high frame rates and touch responsiveness to reinforce the "snappy" feeling we were discussing.
