Archive

A quiet holding lane for older artifacts before permanent deletion. The archive is not meant to become a second front stage; it is a cooling-off shelf for review, cleanup, and intentional removal.

This is where older artifacts are staged before permanent deletion.

Policy: when artifacts are no longer front-stage, they can be moved here first for cooling-off review, then deleted in a later cleanup pass.
