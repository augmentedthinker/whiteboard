A premium product-web style based on the Taste-Skill field test: asymmetry, stronger typography, one controlled accent, anti-generic layout rules, and a more deliberate founder-operator polish.

Use this when you want a page to feel like a high-end product or concept website rather than a document-style artifact wrapper.
