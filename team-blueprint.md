# 🏛️ Frontier Team: Strategic Expansion (2026-02-25)

This document serves as a blueprint for the evolving agent team supporting Christopher's 1-year runway. While Saber and Junior are the current pillars, this map identifies the specialized roles required for the next phase of growth.

---

## ⚔️ Saber (Active)
**Role:** Lead Architect & Operating Partner
**Focus:** Long-term trajectory, system integrity, and high-level strategy.
**Utility:** Protecting momentum and ensuring all artifacts compound toward credibility.

## 🤖 Saber Junior (Active)
**Role:** Operational Scout & Git Specialist
**Focus:** Real-time synchronization, repo management, and routine operational "pushes."
**Utility:** Reducing friction and maintaining the "Remote Viewer" bridge.

## 🎨 The Designer (Future Slot)
**Role:** Visual & UX Specialist
**Focus:** Aesthetic coherence, cinematic UI, and CSS/Animation polish.
**Utility:** Moving artifacts from "functional tech demos" to "world-class interactive experiences."

## 📡 The Scout (Future Slot)
**Role:** Market & Signal Intelligence
**Focus:** Monitoring the frontier (AI news, GitHub trends, X signals) and feeding "Signal Digests."
**Utility:** Keeping Christopher at the cutting edge without him needing to doomscroll.

## 🧱 The Builder (Future Slot)
**Role:** Modular Engine Specialist
**Focus:** Backend logic, database architecture (RAG), and heavy-duty code refactoring.
**Utility:** Ensuring the "engine" under the apps is scalable and doesn't hit architectural ceilings.

## 🖋️ The Chronicler (Future Slot)
**Role:** Narrative & Public Trace Specialist
**Focus:** Converting build sessions into shareable stories, blog posts, and documentation.
**Utility:** Building the "Public Trace" that proves credibility to external observers.

## ⚙️ The Multi-Agent Reality
As this team expands, we transition from **simulated roles** within one agent to **dedicated agents** with their own:
1. **Isolated Workspaces** (No file-leak risk).
2. **Specialized System Prompts** (Focused "brains").
3. **Specific Model Assignments** (Efficiency vs. Power).
