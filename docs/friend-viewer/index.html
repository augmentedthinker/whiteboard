Share Portal

Shared artifacts and documents, presented cleanly.

This portal is the lightweight front door for sharing selected work with collaborators, clients, and friends. It’s meant to feel simple, professional, and easy to navigate — a clean surface for demos, plans, documents, and project materials as this library grows.

What this page is for

A curated place to open specific artifacts without dropping people directly into the broader internal workspace. Over time, this can become the default sharing canvas for proposals, demos, drafts, walkthroughs, and client-facing materials.

Current contents

For now, this portal includes the Rose PRD, the Citation App demo, and the live Legal citation app. The structure is intentionally more universal now, so future items can be added without the page feeling tied to a single person or one-off interaction.
