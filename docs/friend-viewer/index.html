Share Portal

Shared artifacts and documents, presented cleanly.

A lightweight front door for sharing selected work with collaborators, clients, and friends. It should feel simple, trustworthy, and professionally composed — a calm handoff surface rather than a maze of internal pages.

What this page is for

A curated place to open specific artifacts without dropping people directly into the broader internal workspace. Over time, this can become the default sharing canvas for proposals, demos, drafts, walkthroughs, and client-facing materials.

Current contents

For now, this portal includes the Rose PRD, the Citation App demo, and the live Legal citation app. The structure is intentionally broad enough now that future items can be added without the page feeling tied to a single one-off interaction.
